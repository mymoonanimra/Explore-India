About Hampi

Hampi, in Karnataka, was the capital of the Vijayanagara Empire. Known for its ancient ruins, temples, boulders, and historic architecture, Hampi is a paradise for history enthusiasts and photographers.

How to Reach

By Air: Hubli Airport (~165 km) or Bengaluru Airport (~350 km).

By Road: Well connected to Hospet (~13 km), Bangalore, and Goa by bus and taxis.

By Train: Hospet Junction is nearest railway station; frequent trains from major cities.

Top Places to Visit

Vittala Temple Complex: Famous for stone chariot and musical pillars.

Virupaksha Temple: Ancient temple still in use, dedicated to Lord Shiva.

Hampi Bazaar: Historical market street leading to Virupaksha Temple.

Matanga Hill: Best spot for sunrise/sunset with panoramic views of Hampi ruins.

Suggested 2-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Explore Virupaksha Temple, Hampi Bazaar, and Tungabhadra River.

Day 2: Visit Vittala Temple Complex, Matanga Hill, and royal enclosures.
