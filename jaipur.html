About Jaipur

Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan, is known as the Pink City for its terracotta-colored buildings. It is famous for majestic forts, royal palaces, colorful bazaars, and rich culture, blending history and vibrant local life.

How to Reach

By Air: Jaipur International Airport connects to Delhi, Mumbai, Bengaluru, and other major cities.

By Road: Connected by highways to Delhi, Agra, Jodhpur, and Udaipur. Buses and taxis available.

By Train: Jaipur Railway Station has frequent trains to major Indian cities.
