Kochi (Cochin), in Kerala, is a vibrant port city blending colonial heritage, natural beauty, and cultural traditions. Famous for Chinese fishing nets, spice markets, backwaters, and Kathakali performances, Kochi is a must-visit destination in South India.

Top Places to Visit

Fort Kochi: Known for colonial architecture, churches, and Chinese fishing nets.

Mattancherry Palace: Historic palace with Kerala murals and Portuguese influence.

Jewish Synagogue: Oldest synagogue in India, with Belgian chandeliers.

Marine Drive: Scenic promenade along the backwaters.

St. Francis Church: Oldest European church in India, linked with Vasco da Gama.

Suggested 3-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Fort Kochi, Chinese fishing nets, St. Francis Church, Marine Drive.

Day 2: Mattancherry Palace, Jewish Synagogue, Spice Market, evening Kathakali show.

Day 3: Backwater cruise, Bolgatty Palace, shopping at Lulu Mall, departure.
