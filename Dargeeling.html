How to Reach

By Air: Bagdogra Airport (about 70 km) is the nearest airport.

By Train: New Jalpaiguri (NJP) is the nearest major railway station.

By Road: Well-connected by shared jeeps, taxis, and buses from Siliguri, Gangtok, and Kalimpong.

By Toy Train: Darjeeling Himalayan Railway runs scenic rides from NJP/Kurseong.

Top Places to Visit

Tiger Hill: Famous for sunrise views of Mt. Kanchenjunga.

Batasia Loop: Scenic toy train route with war memorial.

Darjeeling Himalayan Railway: UNESCO heritage toy train ride.

Tea Gardens: Explore aromatic Darjeeling tea plantations.

Peace Pagoda: Japanese temple offering serene vibes and hill views.

Suggested 3-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival, toy train ride, Batasia Loop, Darjeeling Mall Road shopping.

Day 2: Early morning at Tiger Hill, visit Ghoom Monastery, Peace Pagoda, tea estates.

Day 3: Himalayan Mountaineering Institute, Zoo, ropeway ride, departure.
