About Ooty

Ooty, also known as Udhagamandalam, is located in the Nilgiri Hills of Tamil Nadu. Known for its tea plantations, botanical gardens, serene lakes, and scenic landscapes, it is a favorite destination for nature lovers and honeymooners.

How to Reach

By Air: Nearest airport is Coimbatore (85 km) with flights from major cities.

By Road: Well connected via buses and taxis from Coimbatore, Bangalore, and Mysore.

By Train: Ooty has the famous Nilgiri Mountain Railway connecting to Mettupalayam.

Top Places to Visit

Ooty Lake: Boating and leisure in a beautiful man-made lake.

Botanical Gardens: Exotic plants, flowers, and well-maintained gardens.

Doddabetta Peak: Highest peak in Nilgiris, offering panoramic views.

Tea Gardens: Lush plantations with guided tours and tea tasting.

Suggested 2-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Visit Ooty Lake, Botanical Gardens, and Tea Gardens.

Day 2: Trek to Doddabetta Peak, explore nearby viewpoints, and local markets.
