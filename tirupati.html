About Tirupati

Tirupati, located in Andhra Pradesh, is one of the holiest pilgrimage cities in India. Famous for the Tirumala Venkateswara Temple on the Tirumala hills, it attracts millions of devotees each year. The town is also known for its scenic hills, waterfalls, and spiritual atmosphere.

How to Reach

By Air: Tirupati Airport (Renigunta) connects to Chennai, Hyderabad, and Bengaluru.

By Train: Tirupati Railway Station has trains from Chennai, Hyderabad, and major cities.

By Road: Well-connected by buses and highways from Chennai (135 km), Bengaluru (250 km), and Hyderabad (560 km).

By Foot: Traditional devotees climb the Alipiri or Srivari Mettu footpaths to Tirumala temple.

Day 1: Darshan at Tirumala Temple, visit Kapila Theertham and Silathoranam.

Day 2: Explore Chandragiri Fort, Sri Kalahasti Temple (36 km from Tirupati), and shopping for prasadam & handicrafts.
