How to Reach Start Point

By Air: Lokpriya Gopinath Bordoloi Airport, Guwahati. Tawang has a helipad but no commercial flights.

By Train: Guwahati Railway Station is the nearest major railway hub.

By Road: NH13 connects Guwahati to Tawang via Tezpur, Bomdila, and Sela Pass.

Key Stops & Attractions

Tezpur: Historic town with Agnigarh Hill and Brahmaputra views.

Bomdila: Scenic town with Buddhist monasteries and viewpoints.

Sela Pass: High-altitude snow-capped pass at 4,170 m.

Dirang Valley: Picturesque valley famous for orange orchards and hot springs.

Tawang Monastery: Largest monastery in India, cultural and spiritual hub.

Madhuri Lake (Sangestar Lake): Crystal-clear lake surrounded by mountains.

Bap Teng Kang Waterfall: Stunning waterfall en route to Tawang.
