About Rameshwaram

Rameshwaram, located in Tamil Nadu, is one of the holiest pilgrimage destinations in India. It is famous for the Ramanathaswamy Temple, sacred rituals, and stunning natural beauty, including pristine beaches and the historic Pamban Bridge.

How to Reach

By Air: Nearest airport is Madurai (175 km), connected to major Indian cities.

By Train: Rameshwaram Railway Station connects with Chennai, Madurai, and Trichy.

By Road: Well-connected via NH49, buses and taxis from Madurai and other Tamil Nadu towns.

Top Places to Visit

Ramanathaswamy Temple: Famous for its long corridors and sacred Shiva lingam.

Pamban Bridge: Historic railway bridge over the sea, an engineering marvel.

Dhanushkodi: Ghost town at the southern tip with serene beaches.

Agnitheertham: Sacred sea near the temple for holy dips.

Kothandaramaswamy Temple: Connected to Ramayana legends.

Suggested 3-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival, visit Ramanathaswamy Temple and Agnitheertham.

Day 2: Excursion to Dhanushkodi, Pamban Bridge, and Kothandaramaswamy Temple.

Day 3: Explore beaches, local markets, and depart.
